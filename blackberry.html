About the Coop

May 2017 - August 2017

From May-August 2017 I had the pleasure to work the the BlackBerry Design team as their UX design intern based in Toronto, ON. During my time I was able to be work alongside experienced creative individuals and be able to collaborate on projects and work individually on my own. Currently, the work that was produced over the time of my co-op has not been published to the public.
